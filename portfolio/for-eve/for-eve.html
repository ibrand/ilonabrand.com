For Eve

A kinetic sculptural exploration of the process of healing. Inspired by a friend's analogy: Imagine a tree growing over concrete. The tree can't remove the concrete, but the tree doesn't stop growing. There is no broken if there is no fixed. There is only a non linear timeline of healing. Growth will not happen straight, it will happen in every direction.

Materials: Acrylic tubing, copper wire, stepper motor, arduino + easy driver, origami paper
